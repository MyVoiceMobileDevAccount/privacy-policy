Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy for MyVoice ("we," "us," or "our") explains how and why we may collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Download and use the MyVoice mobile application, or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Policy;
  • Create or use an optional MyVoice account;
  • Use MyVoice to create, edit, organize, and speak communication cards, groups, and related content; or
  • Contact us or otherwise engage with us in connection with the Services.

MyVoice is an Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) app designed to help users communicate by creating and speaking sentences using customizable cards. Users may create, edit, and organize their own communication cards and groups. Users may also upload images to represent cards.

MyVoice may be used by children or by individuals who rely on AAC for communication. Content created in the app is intended to remain under the user’s or caregiver’s control and may be edited or deleted within the app.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at myvoicemobileapp@gmail.com.

Summary of Key Points

  • We may collect information you provide directly, such as account information if you choose to create an account.
  • We may request access to certain device features, such as storage, camera, or microphone, when needed for app functionality.
  • We use information to operate, maintain, improve, and secure MyVoice and to communicate with users when needed.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Users may have privacy rights depending on where they live, including rights to access, correct, or delete personal information.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal information you disclose to us

In short: We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us.

We collect personal information that you provide when you choose to create an account, contact us, request support, or otherwise interact with the Services.

Depending on how you use MyVoice, the personal information we may collect can include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password or account authentication credentials

Account-related information. Creating an account may be optional. If you choose not to create an account, some features may be unavailable or limited.

User-created content. MyVoice allows users to create and organize communication cards, groups, text, and uploaded images. This content is created and managed by the user or the user’s caregiver.

Sensitive personal information. We do not intentionally collect or process sensitive personal information unless it is voluntarily provided by the user and necessary for the operation of the Services.

Application data and device permissions. If you use our application, we may request access to certain device features, including:

  • Microphone: for speech or audio-related features, if applicable;
  • Storage or photo library: to allow users to upload or manage images and related content;
  • Camera: to capture images for cards or related content, if applicable.

You can manage device permissions through your device settings. If you deny certain permissions, some features of the app may not function properly.

All information you provide should be accurate and up to date. Please let us know if your account information changes.
